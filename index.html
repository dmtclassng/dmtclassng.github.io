Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) is an intensive practical training geared toward expatiating what Digital Marketing is all about, how it works, how it can be used for brand awareness and increase business sales and profit. Digital marketing is soaring in popularity because it is not only a rapidly growing force in the current marketing playing field but it is set to be the future of marketing in general.

Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) aims at enabling Small and Medium Scale business owners know the important of Digital Marketing and how it can be used at attract prospective clients and turning these clients into loyal paying customers. Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) also aims at grooming individuals who will become world class Digital Marketing Experts. Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) is powered by Absolute Hearts Media, Nigeria's foremost Digital Marketing and Online Media company.
