Overview

Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) is an intensive practical training geared toward expatiating what Digital Marketing is all about, how it works, how it can be used for brand awareness and increase business sales and profit.

Digital marketing is soaring in popularity because it is not only a rapidly growing force in the current marketing playing field but it is set to be the future of marketing in general.

Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) aims at enabling Small and Medium Scale business owners know the important of Digital Marketing and how it can be used at attract prospective clients and turning these clients into loyal paying customers.

Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) also aims at grooming individuals who will become world class Digital Marketing Experts.

Digital Marketing Training Class (DMTClassNG) is powered by Absolute Hearts Media, Nigeria's foremost Digital Marketing and Online Media company.

Courses

Courses to be taught at the training include:
1. Introduction to Digital and Social Media Marketing
a. SMS & Mobile Marketing
b. Information Marketing
c. Affiliate Marketing

2. SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING:
a. Gaining Marketing edge with Social Media Marketing
b. Learning how to deploy social media tools for business profit
c. The Social Media selling approach
d. Business opportunities in Social Media Marketing
e. Practicals on how to launch effective Social Media Sponsored Ads (Campaigns).
f. Intensive practical session on Social Media marketing, how to build social media pages to get huge followership & social media optimization
g. Understanding Social Media analytics to measure business growth

3. SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION
a. Understanding keywords attributes
b. Understanding keywords distribution.

4. Blogging & Content Marketing
a. Introduction to Blogging and Content Marketing
b. Practicals on setting up a professional blog
c. Managing a professional blog
d. Tools needed for Blogging
e. Monetizing your blog (making money from your blog)
f. Using Blogging a grow your business sales and awareness

5. In-depth revelation on opportunities in digital marketing and the digital space at large and how to start a profitable career in any of them (including social media marketing, Mobile Marketing/Bulk SMS, Blogging, Web Designing, SEO, Affiliate Marketing, Information Marketing, Email Marketing, etc).

6. How to build your own Digital Marketing Agency in 30 days and how to make Digital Marketing Agency profitable, bankable and trustworthy.
